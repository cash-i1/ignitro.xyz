Enviroment Up!

Enviroment Up! is a game where you go up and up and up for ever.

How the game works

In Enviroment Up!, you go up forever, jumping on flatforms automatically. If you touch pollution, you will be pushed down and get +1 pollution score (which is bad) but if you touch a non-pollution, you are boosted up and get +1 non-pollution score. If you have more pollution score than non-pollution score, the screen slowly starts to turn red and fade to black unless you get your pollution score higher.

Trivia

  • Enviroment Up! was made for a school project.
  • Enviroment Up! is inspired by doodle jump
  • Enviroment Up! is made with the godot game engine
